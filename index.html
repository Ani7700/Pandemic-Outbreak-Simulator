    How this works

    Each local authority is shaded by its real Q3 2025–26 vaccination coverage (UKHSA COVER programme). Population data comes from ONS mid-2024 estimates.

    The outbreak simulator uses a 50-run stochastic SDE measles model (Chen & Zilka 2025), calibrated on measles parameters regardless of which vaccine is selected for the map view. The slider lets you explore hypothetical coverage scenarios for the measles simulation.

    Trajectories are pre-computed per NHS region across 11 coverage levels (50%–98%), then scaled to each local authority's population.

    MMR2 & MMR1: UKHSA 5-year cohort table (T7_UTLA5y). MenB, PCV, Rotavirus: 12-month cohort table (T5_UTLA12m) — 149 UTLAs; 3 shire counties not separately reported. DTaP: 5-year cohort table (T7_UTLA5y).
